Naruto (anime series)

Naruto is a story of friendship, war, piece among many others. For me, this has shown how being to cope with challenges and unpleasant situations is an essential path of achieving dreams and realizing goals.

The show has quite many episodes thus requires commitment to be watched. However, it can be optimized by avoiding fillers. This service can help with it.

The Wolf of Wall Street

"There's no nobility in poverty" ©

The story of Jordan Belfort illustrates how success makes one's head dizzy. Of course, showing up "in the back of the limo, wearing a $2000 suit and a $40,000 gold fuckin' watch" © feels good, but attaining success materially is not the end of the road. One needs to handle it diligently too.

Martin Scorsese skillfully tells the story of an American entrepreneur Jordan Ross Belfort. Although the plot most likely exaggerates it, the movie is thrilling to watch!

The Sopranos

Cinematography has addressed the topic of Mafia countless times, but this show does it in a unique & memorable way.

For me, the Sorpanos firstly and foremostly is about its characters. After a couple of episodes of Tony and the co sitting at the table and having their swearing-filled small talk, one can almost feel being next to Paulie when he cracks another joke.

This show is purely for entertainment, yet of very high quality.
